# BAB Framework

> Source: [Juuzt AI - BAB Framework](https://juuzt.ai/knowledge-base/prompt-frameworks/the-bab-framework/)

## Framework Overview

The BAB Framework (Before, After, Bridge) was originally designed as a copywriting formula and has been successfully adapted for AI prompt engineering. Like PAS (Pain, Agitate, Solution) and AIDA (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action), BAB was created to structure persuasive text but has found new life in guiding AI models to deliver precise and impactful responses.

Its emphasis on storytelling and emotional resonance makes it an ideal tool for crafting prompts that connect user needs with actionable solutions.

---

## Three Core Components

| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Before** | Paint the user's problem or challenge vividly |
| **After** | Envision the ideal solution or state, showing the transformation they desire |
| **Bridge** | Connect the problem and solution, highlighting how AI or a specific product/service provides the desired outcome |

---

## BAB vs AIDA Comparison

| Aspect | BAB | AIDA |
|--------|-----|------|
| **Focus** | Emotion-driven storytelling | Attention, interest, desire, and action |
| **Best for** | Persuasion through problem-solution narratives | Structured, step-by-step conversion processes |
| **Flexibility** | Excellent for emotionally charged copy | Great for capturing and converting leads |

## Prompt Examples

### Example 1: Meal Subscription Service

**Scenario**: Creating a promotional campaign for a meal subscription service

```
Before: Busy professionals struggle to find time to cook healthy meals after work.
After: Imagine enjoying chef-prepared meals delivered right to your door every evening.
Bridge: Offer a weekly subscription that provides healthy, ready-to-eat meals at an affordable price.
```

### Example 2: Fitness App Marketing

**Scenario**: Creating a marketing message for a fitness app

```
Before: People struggle to stay consistent with their fitness goals.
After: Picture a personalized fitness plan that adapts to your schedule and keeps you motivated.
Bridge: Introduce our AI-powered fitness app that designs custom workouts and tracks progress.
```

### Example 3: Online Learning Platform

**Scenario**: Promoting an online learning platform

```
Before: Many students find it hard to access affordable and quality education.
After: Imagine mastering new skills anytime, anywhere, at your own pace.
Bridge: Offer our platform with thousands of expert-led courses starting at just $10/month.
```

### Example 4: Mental Health Support App

**Scenario**: Designing an ad for a mental health support app

```
Before: Many people feel overwhelmed and alone dealing with stress and anxiety.
After: Imagine feeling calm and supported every day with expert mental health guidance.
Bridge: Download our app for access to licensed therapists and mindfulness exercises 24/7.
```

### Example 5: Travel Planning Service

**Scenario**: Creating a campaign for a travel planning service

```
Before: Planning a vacation is stressful and time-consuming for busy individuals.
After: Imagine enjoying a stress-free vacation planned perfectly to your preferences.
Bridge: Use our travel planning service to book your dream vacation effortlessly.
```
