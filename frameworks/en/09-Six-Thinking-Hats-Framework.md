## Prompt Examples

### Example 1: Product Innovation

**Scenario**: Brainstorming ideas for a new kitchen gadget

```
White Hat: Analyze current market data and competitor products.
Red Hat: Consider emotional responses customers might have to the gadget.
Black Hat: Identify potential risks and challenges in developing the gadget.
Yellow Hat: Highlight the benefits and opportunities the gadget offers.
Green Hat: Brainstorm creative features or uses for the gadget.
Blue Hat: Summarize insights and plan the next steps for product development.
```

### Example 2: Marketing Strategy

**Scenario**: Designing a campaign for an eco-friendly product

```
White Hat: Gather data on the target market and current eco-friendly trends.
Red Hat: Reflect on the emotional appeal of the product to consumers.
Black Hat: Assess potential challenges and obstacles in the campaign.
Yellow Hat: Emphasize the positive impacts and benefits of the product.
Green Hat: Develop innovative marketing ideas and unique selling points.
Blue Hat: Organize the campaign timeline and delegate tasks accordingly.
```

### Example 3: Business Expansion

**Scenario**: Planning to expand your business into a new market

```
White Hat: Research the new market's size, demographics, and competition.
Red Hat: Consider your team's feelings and intuitions about entering this market.
Black Hat: Identify potential risks and challenges of the expansion.
Yellow Hat: Highlight the benefits and opportunities of expanding into this market.
Green Hat: Brainstorm creative strategies to differentiate your business.
Blue Hat: Summarize insights and structure next steps for the expansion.
```

### Example 4: Change Management

**Scenario**: Implementing a major change in your organization

```
White Hat: Gather data on the current state and reasons for change.
Red Hat: Reflect on the emotions and sentiments of employees towards the change.
Black Hat: Identify potential resistance and challenges in the change process.
Yellow Hat: Highlight the benefits and positive outcomes of the change.
Green Hat: Brainstorm strategies to facilitate the change and engage employees.
Blue Hat: Plan the change implementation steps and monitor progress.
```
